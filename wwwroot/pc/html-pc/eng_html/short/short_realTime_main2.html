How to use?

Choosing Rental Period
  • Reservation is available up to 60 days prior to the pick up date.
    (Up to 90 days prior for Incheon International Airport)
  • Minimum 24 hours of rental period is required to apply discount
Choosing a Location
  • Please check operation hours of the location that you are willing to pick up a vehicle
  • Locations cannot be chosen if the location cannot serve you with your selected rental period and car model.
  • Additional charge will be added if return location is different from pick up location.
Choosing a Car
  • You can choose either General (choose a specific model), or Mystery Deal (choose car group only)
  • Mystery Deal is available for members only.
