Information for Point use

Point

  • Membership points can be used when you have more than 50,000 points.
  • Points are allowed to be used only for self-drive rental which are less than 30 days rentals.
  • During the booking process, check your remaining points and if it’s over 50,000 points, Please check and use your points you like
  • If you make payment of rental charge with points, payment covered by points will not be credited

Information of Insurance

Mainland

  • You only have to pay between 100,000 ~ 500,000 KRW have the car damage covered.
  • The option fee is different depending on the category of options for deductibles for CDW
  • Categories of deductibles
    • Korea-made cars : Zero Excess, 50,000won, 300,000won​
    • Foreign made cars : 300,000 KRW (single deductible system adopted)​

Jeju Island Categories of deductibles

  • Korea-made cars : full-waiver system, 300,000 KRW
  • Under full-waiver system, no customer contribution regardless of the amount of car damage, and liability for car damage is covered
  • If the car repair cost is below the deductible as opted for relevant category of deductibles
  • settlement will be made on an actual-cost basis, Otherwise, you only have to pay the highest deductibles as opted.

Jeju Island Categories of deductibles

  • Customers are liable to pay repair cost in connection with any damage (loss or destruction)
  • Compensation for an Inactive Vehicle : Customers are responsible for operational loss even with CDW registration in case of the repair cost exceeds 50% of the vehicle’s residual value. The compensation is 50% of rental fare corresponding to the repairing period.
  • If you have selected an CDW, and option for customers, any own damage to the vehicle is covered
