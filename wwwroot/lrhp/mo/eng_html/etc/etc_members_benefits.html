Points Redemption Criteria

If 50,000 or more Rent-A-Car poin4ts have accumulated, it is possible to cash out points for use towards car rental at any LOTTE Rent-A-Car location.

  • LOTTE rent-a-car points can be redeemed if 50,000 or more have accumulated.
  • Points redemption is only available for general vehicle rentals for less than 30days.
  • Points redemption is only available towards rental fee. (CDW, DS, one-way rental, refueling service charge not available)
  • Please check your points balance when making reservations, and mark the box if you would like to use your points and if your points are 50,000 or more.
    Type in the amount of points you would like to redeem.
  • Points Redemption will take place on the day of rental, at the rental location.
  • If points are redeemed towards the rental fee, redeemed amount does not apply to points accumulation.
  • However, this only applies to the points accumulated before January 12, 2015.If 50,000P or more rental points are accumulated, the remaining points may be used.
