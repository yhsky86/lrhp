Terms of service

Rental Eligibility

    • Small/medium/van of 7 or 9 passenger capacity – Over 21 years of age, with 1 year of longer driving experience.
    • Van of 10 passenger capacity – Over 21 years of age, with 1 years of longer driving experience.
    • If you seek registration of a second driver, he or she must accompany you carrying his/her international driving permit at pick-up
    • Passport and International Driving Permit are REQUIRED for identification check.
    • If the driver does not meet the eligibility as above, car rental may be rejected on the site. So, please check the eligibility beforehand.

International driving permit

    • International driving permit(must be presented at pick-up)
      • International Driving Permit must be valid under the Road Traffic Act
      • Please check the member countries under the
      • Geneva & Vienna Conventions
    • For foreign customer, only those with International Driving Permit(Grade B– vehicles with 9 or less passengers capacity) can rent the car.
    • For customers holding US passport, we only accept International Driving Permit issued by AAA(The American Automobile Association).
    • Please check the availability of your International Driving Permit in your country before making the reservation.
    • If you are not from any countries under the Geneva or Vienna Conventions, you are not allowed to rent a car in Korea.

Insurance

    • All of the cars are insured with comprehensive insurance.
    • By signing the agreement at the branch, renter will be accepted to the comprehensive insurance include Third Party liability(Unlimited), Property damage(20 million KRW); and personal Injuries(15 million KRW).

Payment Terms

    • The payment for rental fee should be made by the main driver’s own credit card. (No debit card, cash or dollar are accepted for payment)
    • If not, we accept main driver’s companion credit card who will be with the main driver throughout the rental period.
    • If you fail to show up at the branch by the reserved time without any prior notice, your reservation will be cancelled without advance warning.
